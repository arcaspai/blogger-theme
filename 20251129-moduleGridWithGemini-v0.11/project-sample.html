The Challenge

Modern cities are often perceived as chaotic and disconnected. Our goal was to create a visual identity that finds order within chaos, using a brutalist grid system to represent the underlying structure of urban life.

We focused on the interplay between concrete textures and sharp digital overlays. The challenge was to make "Brutalism" feel approachable and functional for a public service interface, ensuring accessibility while maintaining a strong aesthetic character.

Grid System

  • 12-Column Responsive Layout
  • Modular Typography Scale
  • High Contrast Color Palette

The Solution

By implementing a strict modular grid, we allowed for flexible content scaling across all devices. The use of #E3AEFF as a highlight color breaks the monotony of the gray-scale architectural photos, guiding the user's eye to critical information.
